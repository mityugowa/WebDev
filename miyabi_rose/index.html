About Miyabi

Miyabi is a multicolored rose that starts as a medium pink only to open and show light pink interior.
Her petals are strong and form star shapes as she opens.
Very hardy rose, great for traveling and for vase work.

  • Color: Peach
  • Secondary Color: Medium Pink
  • Bloom Form: Rosette
  • Head Size: Large
  • Petal Count: 100
  • Fragrance Intensity: 0 of 5
  • Fragrance: Not applicable
  • Vase Life: 10 Days
  • Origin: Japan
  • Length: 40-60 cm
  • Side Shoots: No
  • Bloom Opening Habit: 50%

  • Substitute: Juliet, Princess Suki
